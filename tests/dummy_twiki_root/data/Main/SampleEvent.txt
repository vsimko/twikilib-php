---++ Production

Do not erase this table, each column serves its purpose:
   * *Role* quick link for the further sections in the topic
   * *Who* person responsible for the particular section
   * *Accepted* the responsible person should accept (change &ldquo;no&rdquo; to &ldquo;yes&rdquo;) his/her role after receiving a notification email from the event creator
   * *When* specification of date/time if e.g. photo will take place only at certain time form the whole event
